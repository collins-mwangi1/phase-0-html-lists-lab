• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarela
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. spread the butter on bread and frying pan
  2. place bread on frying pan and fly
  3. add cheese on top of bread
  4. cover with second slice of bread
  5. turn over and fry for two minutes
